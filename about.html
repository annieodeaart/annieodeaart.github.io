About The Artist

Art is a passion I have grown to love more and more over the last few years of my life. The ability art has to help me express my emotions in a personal way is something I value about it. I love that I can dedicate endless amounts of time to perfecting my work until it is exactly what I want it to be. It allows me to draw on my creativity and create art that represents who I am and what I love.
